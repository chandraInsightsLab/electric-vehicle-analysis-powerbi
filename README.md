# electric-vehicle-dashboard-analysis-powerbi

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Electric Vehicle Dashboard Analysis** project provides an interactive visualization of electric vehicle (EV) adoption,
market trends, and vehicle distribution. The dashboard enables users to explore key insights such as total EV registrations,
battery electric vehicles (BEVs), plug-in hybrid electric vehicles (PHEVs), manufacturer performance, model popularity, and 
geographic distribution.

The dashboard was developed using **Power BI** to help stakeholders, researchers, and business analysts make data-driven 
decisions.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze the growth of electric vehicles.
* Compare Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs).
* Identify the top-performing EV manufacturers.
* Discover the most popular EV models.
* Analyze EV distribution across states and cities.
* Understand vehicle eligibility for Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle (CAFV) incentives.
* Provide an interactive dashboard for business insights.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Total Electric Vehicles
* Total BEVs and PHEVs
* BEV vs PHEV Percentage
* Average Electric Range
* Top EV Manufacturers
* Top Vehicle Models
* EV Distribution by State
* EV Distribution by City
* CAFV Eligibility Analysis
* Interactive Filters (City, Electric Utility, Electric Vehicle Type)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Battery Electric Vehicles account for the majority of total EV registrations.
* Tesla leads the market in total registered electric vehicles.
* Recent model years show a significant increase in EV adoption.
* A small number of manufacturers dominate the EV market.
* Electric vehicle registrations are concentrated in a few major states.
* Most registered EVs qualify for Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle incentives.

## 📌 Dashboard KPIs

| ---------------------- | -----------------------------------------------|
| Total EVs              | Total registered electric vehicles             |
| Total BEVs             | Battery Electric Vehicles                      |
| Total PHEVs            | Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles               |
| Average Electric Range | Average driving range of EVs                   |
| Top Manufacturer       | Manufacturer with highest registrations        |
| Top Model              | Most registered EV model                       |
| CAFV Eligible Vehicles | Vehicles eligible for incentives               |
| Total % of BEVs        | Percentage of Battery Electric Vehicles        |
